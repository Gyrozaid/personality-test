Which personality test would you like to take?


Key
SA - strongly agree
A - agree
N - neutral
D - disagree
SD - strongly disagree


Extraversion - how outgoing you are, how much positive emotion you feel
Agreeableness - how polite you are, how compassionate and empathetic you are
Conscientiousness - how hard working you are, how orderly and clean you are
Neuroticism - how much negative emotion you feel
Openness - how willing you are to experience new experiences, how often you think abstractly
